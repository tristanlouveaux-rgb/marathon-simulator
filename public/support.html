Adaptive triathlon and marathon training for iOS

What is Mosaic?

Mosaic is a personal training plan app for triathletes and marathon runners. It connects to Strava to read your activity history, calibrates your fitness benchmarks, and generates adaptive weekly training plans around your race goals.

Disconnect Strava

To revoke Mosaic's access to your Strava data, go to your Strava settings, find Mosaic under "My Apps", and click Revoke Access. Your data will be deleted within 48 hours.

Delete your data

To request deletion of all data Mosaic holds about you, email tristan.louveaux@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data".
